About Me 🌟

I was born in the serene village of Ribdikot Ward No. 07, Palpa, a place where my earliest memories are deeply rooted in simplicity, warmth, and the natural beauty of the hills. Those beginnings instilled in me values of humility, perseverance, and gratitude that continue to guide me today. 🌿

My academic journey led me to the bustling city of Butwal, where I completed my schooling, +2 level, and Bachelor's degree. It was here that I not only gained education but also discovered my passions, built lifelong friendships, and cultivated dreams that fuel my ambitions. 🌸

Today, I serve as a Documentation Officer at Right And Associates Education Consultancy, where I dedicate myself to guiding and assisting students in preparing their documents for studying abroad. Nothing brings me greater joy than helping others take a step closer to their dreams and knowing I play a part in their success. ✨

Beyond work, I find happiness in life's simple joys — exploring new places, capturing fleeting moments through photography, and immersing myself in the calming embrace of nature. 🌍 Every journey, big or small, reminds me that life is a beautiful adventure meant to be cherished and shared. 💫

My Work Experience

📑 Documentation Officer

At Right & Associates Education Consultancy, I have been working for the past two years, supporting students in achieving their dreams of studying abroad. As a Documentation Officer, my role blends precision, mentorship, and advocacy. I ensure every student's application is accurate, complete, and aligned with visa requirements. From preparing and reviewing documents to coordinating with institutions and overseeing visa processes, I work diligently to remove obstacles and transform aspirations into opportunities. ✨

About Right & Associates 🌍

Right & Associates is a well-established education consultancy with over two decades of experience, founded in 2001 in Kathmandu, Nepal. Since then, it has grown into a trusted name in international education, expanding its presence across Nepal — with branches in Kathmandu, Butwal, Chitwan, Pokhara, and more — and internationally, including offices in Australia.

The consultancy provides end-to-end support for students aspiring to study abroad, offering services such as career counseling, course and university selection, documentation and visa assistance, along with pre-departure and post-arrival support. This ensures students feel guided and confident at every step of their journey.

At its core, Right & Associates believes in putting students first. It emphasizes tailored guidance, transparency, and a commitment to turning dreams into reality. Counselors and staff receive regular training and certification, ensuring they uphold high standards of professionalism, accuracy, and care for each student.

With a strong network of partner universities and colleges across countries like Australia, the USA, Canada, the UK, and beyond, Right & Associates helps students find the right pathway based on their background, goals, and aspirations. Its global reach makes it a reliable companion for those looking to transform their educational journeys. 🌹

🎓 Student Support & Guidance

Beyond documentation, I also engage directly with students — providing personalized one-on-one guidance, answering their questions, and helping them build the confidence they need to pursue international education successfully. Each student's success story inspires me to keep making a difference. 🌟

🤝 Community Engagement

I actively contribute to workshops and outreach programs, helping young learners explore the possibilities of global education. By sharing knowledge about visa processes, documentation, and preparation, I aim to inspire the next generation to dream big and pursue opportunities beyond borders. 💫

Stories

My First Day in Butwal

I still remember the mix of excitement and nervousness I felt when I first arrived in Butwal. The bustling city was so different from the peaceful hills of Palpa. Everything moved faster, and there were so many new faces. But amidst the chaos, I found kindness in strangers who became friends and opportunities that shaped my future.

A Student's Success Story

One of my most memorable experiences at Right & Associates was helping a student who had been rejected twice by other consultancies. We worked tirelessly on her application, highlighting her unique strengths and passion. When she received her acceptance letter, we both cried tears of joy. That moment reminded me why I love what I do.

Finding Beauty in Small Moments

During a weekend trip to the countryside, I witnessed the most beautiful sunrise I've ever seen. The way the light filtered through the trees and danced on the dew-covered flowers reminded me that sometimes the most extraordinary moments are hidden in ordinary days. I try to carry that lesson with me always.
